Taking care of cats is my cherished hobby, and I have a lot of cats to care for. However, my absolute favorites are Whiskas, Ben, Yoru, and Dubbu. Each of them holds a special place in my heart, bringing joy and companionship to my life.



WHISKAS


Whiskas is a sweet three-year-old cat with soft grey fur and a black stripe. He's loving, playful, and brings joy to my home every day. He's my cherished companion, making wonderful memories together.


BEN


My cute white cat named Ben. He's three years old, just like Whiskas. Ben has long, fluffy hair and weighs 5kg. He's playful and affectionate. I love playing with him and cherish his presence in my life.


YORU


Yoru, my youngest cat, is just one year old with sleek black fur. He's the most spoiled and mischievous, yet incredibly affectionate. His playful antics bring joy to our home, and I adore him.


DUBBU


Dubbu, my oldest cat, is almost 6 years old with short fur. She resembles Ben but quieter and easier to care for. Her gentle nature brings calmness to our home, making her a cherished companion.
